Dark Chocolate Mousse with Freshly Stirred Raspberries
Crème Anglaise
  • 370g milk
  • 150g sugar
  • 160g egg yolks

Mix everything together in a stainless steel saucepan and heat while stirring until the cream begins to thicken. Strain the cream through a sieve.

Dark Chocolate Mousse
  • 6 sheets of gelatin
  • 600g whipping cream
  • 300g dark chocolate

Soak the gelatin sheets in water. Whip the cream to a loose foam and place it in the refrigerator. Warm the chocolate in the microwave to 55 degrees Celsius, then blend the dark chocolate and crème anglaise into a smooth cream. Lift up the gelatin sheets and melt them in the microwave to 40-50 degrees Celsius, then stir them into the chocolate cream. Fold the whipped cream into the chocolate cream to form a smooth mousse. Place in piping bags.

Freshly Stirred Raspberries
  • 500g raspberries
  • 100g sugar

Mix together and refrigerate.
